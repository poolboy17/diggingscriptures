## Understanding the Coin Hoard
 
You might be wondering what a coin hoard is and why it is significant. In simple terms, a coin hoard refers to a collection of coins that were deliberately hidden, often with the intent of retrieving them later. These troves can offer incredible insights into the economy, culture, and beliefs of the society that minted them.
